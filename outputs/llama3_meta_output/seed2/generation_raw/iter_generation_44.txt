\textbf{Conclusion} \\
In conclusion, our system, $\mathcal{R}$ead, $\mathcal{R}$evise, $\mathcal{R}$epeat (\textsc{$\mathcal{R}3$}), demonstrates significant time savings and improved revision quality compared to human writers. 
The system's ability to learn from user feedback and adapt to writing styles is demonstrated, and the average number of revisions required to achieve high-quality text revisions decreases significantly as the revision depth increases, from $4.29$ revisions at the $1$st revision depth to $4.12$ revisions at the $3$rd$ revision depth.
We hope that our work will contribute to the development of more effective writing assistants in the future, and we look forward to exploring the potential applications of $\mathcal{R}$ead, $\mathcal{R}$evise, $\mathcal{R}$epeat (\textsc{$\mathcal{R}3$}) in various fields.
