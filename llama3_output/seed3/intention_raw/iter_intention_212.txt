Facial features: The first version of LLaMA had an exceptionally strong performance with respect to positive sentiment and a weak performance with respect to negative sentiment. It was also ineffective with respect to formality and irony. However, this was quickly corrected by our fine-tuning approach, which was able to learn how to control these styles more.

It is also important to acknowledge that these style-controlled models were not successful in controlling all styles simultaneously. However, our approach appears to control multiple styles much more effectively than the PPLM or GPT-4 approaches. We also observe that controlling target styles is not sufficient for the 2-style control; the 1-style control is much more effective than the 2-style control. This suggests that there may be inherent limitations to the ability of LLMs to control multiple styles simultaneously.
