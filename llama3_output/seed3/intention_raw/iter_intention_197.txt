\subsection{Data}
We used the \textsc{Pubmed} dataset \cite{sen2008collective}, which consists of scientific journals collected from the PubMed database. The data representation used in our study includes the title, abstract, introduction, related work, conclusion, and citations from the LLM-assisted and GPT-4-generated texts. The title, abstract, introduction, and conclusion represent the context of the original related work, while the related work section represents the original related work section.
