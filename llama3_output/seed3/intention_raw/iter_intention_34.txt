\rubric{Contributions: }Our contributions are as follows:
\begin{itemize}
    \item We present \system, an AI-assisted tool for organizing citations and composing Related Work sections;
    \item We provide an evaluation framework that compares AI-generated text to human-written text in terms of diversity and consistency; and
    \item We Contribute to the ongoing discourse on the role of LLMs in academic writing, highlighting the potential benefits and pitfalls of their use.
\end{itemize}
