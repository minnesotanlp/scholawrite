\begin{enumerate}
    \item  Main findings\anonymous{anonymous}
    We find that artificial data should be used with caution for  
unconventional, nuanced tasks. When using LLMs to label data, labels for controversial data points will likely not reflect the nuance of the human population's views. Similarly, LLMs generating preference data take a more formulaic, less nuanced approach than humans do. LLMs generating text social contexts can have a tone and application of style that is unnatural.
