\section{Conclusion}
The conversation between multiple agents is a very interesting and dynamic form of communication, and it has been shown that it can be used for a wide range of knowledge-intensive and complex tasks. 
However, the simulation of multiple agents through the conversation has become a difficult challenge, because it is hard to make agents to behave in a certain way in the simulation.
But the research on the agents' behavior in the simulation is still in the early stage, and there is a wide range of possibilities for the future development.
It is also important to note that our work is focused on the specific social dimension of formality, and it is not a comprehensive study of all kinds of artificial data. 
However, our approach of modeling the agents' personas as the agents' attributes can be more effective in handling the different kinds of  in a wide range of applications, such as robotics, agent selection, and so on.
