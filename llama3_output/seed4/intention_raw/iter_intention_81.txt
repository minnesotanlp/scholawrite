Mitigation strategies : 

To mitigate these issues, we suggest enhancing data quality and diversity by incorporating domain-specific information and examples into LLM training, in order to expose LLMs to a broader range of perspectives. We propose refining model interaction and responsiveness to align LLM responses more closely with humans, especially in social and informal contexts. This involves calibrating LLMs to better understand and reflect varied emotional expressions and contextual appropriateness, aiming to reduce discrepancies in complex societal language constructs.
