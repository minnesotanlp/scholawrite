One important aspect of studying reward models is that the reward model should not only accurately predict the target style, but also be able to generate a complete sentence or prompt. This is because any good reward model should be able to generate a completion that is as close to the human prompt as possible; this would be referred to as a "high quality" reward model. However, this aspect has not been fully studied, and it is an area of future work.
