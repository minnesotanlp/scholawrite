\subsection{Results}
Figure \ref{fig:fig8} shows the results comparing the text, image, and motif modalities with the desired  on the CORA dataset. We observe that with the smaller and simpler CORA graph, the image modality has higher accuracy and lower denial rate compared to the text modality. We also observe that the motif modality has the highest accuracy and the lowest denial rate on the CORA dataset. 

Table \ref{tab:accuracy_tab} shows the accuracy of node classification across different datasets and modalities. We observe that with the smaller and simpler CITeseer graph, the image modality has higher accuracy than the text modality. We also observe that the motif modality has the highest accuracy on the CORA dataset. On the other hand, with the larger and more  from Pubmed, we observe that the image modality has higher accuracy than the text modality. We also observe that the motif modality has the lowest denial rate and higher accuracy on the CORA dataset.
