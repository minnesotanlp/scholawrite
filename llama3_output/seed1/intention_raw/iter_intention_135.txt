bible/religion - The religion is a distinct, meaningful aspect of human culture and society. It is a source of inspiration, guidance, and identity. Religion forms strong social bonds and often defines an individual's moral code and values. Religion also influences how an individual thinks about the world, including their worldview and understanding of certain events.
