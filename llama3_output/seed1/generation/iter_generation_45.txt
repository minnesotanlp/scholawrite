\textbf{Our contributions}: Specifically, the contributions of our work include:
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep,topsep=5pt,leftmargin=*]
    \item An extensive comparison of graph-structured data and LLMs' understanding of text modality, highlighting current challenges and research gaps in this area.
    \item In-depth study of graph task types and their difficulties, with a new task difficulty classification system to aid the community in assessing the complexity of graph problems.
    \item The creation of the GraphTMI benchmark, which provides a comprehensive assessment of the impact of graph structure, sampling methods, and the level of noise in the modality on node classification tasks.
    \item Exploring the effects of graph structure on different LLMs, highlighting the importance of graph understanding in LLMs to improve their performance on graph-related tasks.
    \item A new graph benchmark (GraphTMI) with a Task hierarchy consisting of graphs, associated prompts, and reference graphs, which can be used by the community to create more challenging tasks.
    
\end{itemize}
