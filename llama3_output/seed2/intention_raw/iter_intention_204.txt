/if 2-style models are to be useful in a wide variety of situations, it is necessary to include fine-tuning in the fine-tuning process. It is also necessary to include the option for including a third style in the fine-tuning process, since including a third style could improve the performance of the model. We find that including a third style improves the performance of the model in 1 out of 1, 1 out of 3, and 1 out of 5 experiments. We also find that 2-style models are more difficult to learn to 1-style models. This is likely because the 2-style model has to learn more styles at once, or because the 2-style models are further away from the simple 1-style model.
