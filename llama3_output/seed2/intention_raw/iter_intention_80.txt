\paragraph{Hypothesis}
The first order effects primarily concern the direct outcomes observed in the synthetic dataset. We hypothesize that the inherent limitations of LLMs become apparent when they are put to the test of understanding complex instructions or when they generate text that may not align with the expected outputs of the tasks.
