\subsection{Combinations of Two Styles}
We first fine-tune RL models for all four possible combinations of sentiment and formality. We choose these two styles since their respective discriminators have the highest F1 scores (Table~\ref{table:discrims}).  We use the GradWt reward formulation, as it was the most successful reward formulation based on the evaluation in Section~\ref{sec:reward_eval}. We consider the generations of the resulting models on our evaluation prompts, as well as the results of other approaches for multi-style controlled generations. For the results of these two-style models with respect to style accuracy, see Table~\ref{tab:precision_base-rl-pplm}; for a sample of generations produced by these models, see Appendix\ref{sec:appendix} Table~\ref{tab:generations}.
