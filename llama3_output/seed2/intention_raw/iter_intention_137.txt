\subsection{Comparison with PPLM}
We extend PPLM to learn some basic tricks by adjusting the KL divergence with the reference model. We find that this technique results in less effective style control. We hypothesize that this may be due to  that the models have a non-integer  and that the KL divergence with the reference model is not properly controlled for.
