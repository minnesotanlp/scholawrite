To mitigate this issue, we suggest enhancing data quality and diversity by incorporating domain-specific information and examples into LLM training, in order to expose LLMs to a broader range of perspectives. We also propose refining model interaction and responsiveness to align LLM responses more closely with humans, especially in social and informal contexts. Additionally, it is important to include high-level human feedback and validation in the training of LLMs, so that they can more accurately reflect human opinions and understanding.
