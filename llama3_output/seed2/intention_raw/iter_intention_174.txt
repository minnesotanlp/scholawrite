\textbf{Our contributions}: Specifically, the contributions of our work are the following:

\begin{itemize}
  \item  An extensive study of graph-structure prompting across multiple modalities for LLMs.
  \item Exploring the impact of graph structure, sampling methods, and no. of hops on node prediction performance
  \item In-depth study factors affecting text, image, and motif modalities for encoding graphs.
  \item A new graph benchmark (GraphTMI) with a Task hierarchy consisting of graphs, and associated encodings in all encodings to aid the community in studying the effects of graph structure on LLM prompting further.
  
\end{itemize}

\textbf{what do we see from our results?}
\textcolor{red}{image greater than text - task hierarchy}
Our empirical results show that, while LLMs are getting better at handling graph data, they still have a lot of improving to do if they are to catch up with models that are specifically designed to work with graphs.
