Credentialing  \textbf{the importance of responsible AI}
As various LLMs are employed to create a wide range of data, the need as well as the risks associated with using these datasets have risen significantly. It is crucial to understand the limitations and biases inherent in LLM-generated data as these affect the quality and diversity of the data used for training. Moreover, the high error rates in instructions generated by LLMs raise concerns about the reliability and accuracy of the outputs produced by models trained on these datasets. 

\textbf{the risks of using LLM-generated data}
The growing dependence on such data for its convenience and lower costs and speculations of GPT-5 training on its own generated data \footnote{Link to tweet: https://shorturl.at/kmAY2}, raise alarming bells about an emerging cycle of AI-reliant solely on artificial inputs.
